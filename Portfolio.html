How I Work

My Approach

01
Systems before campaigns
I tend to set up qualification frameworks and signal capture before scaling outreach — it helps make sure effort goes to the right accounts.
02
Pipeline over activity
I try to connect every programme to a commercial outcome and track it through to closed-won, not just lead volume.
03
Pilot, then scale
I prefer to test a concept locally first — the AI workshop in Korea is one example — and expand once there's something proven to build on.
04
Cross-market execution
Leading marketing across Korea, Japan, China, and Taiwan, I adapt programmes to each market's dynamics while keeping the overall strategy aligned.
